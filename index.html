Company's Timeline

  • 1993

    In 1993, TOPWAY FAREAST LTD. was established in Hongkong and founded by Mr. Leung Lui Ming. The company focusing on financial platform investment and banking facilities that accessible to Asian and European market.

    TOPWAY, in collaboration with Euro Exim performs facilitation of Bank Instruments namely DLC (Domestic Letter of Credits), ULC (USANCE Letter of Credit), SBLC (Standby Letter of Credit) and other Letter of Credits. Since then, TOPWAY has been working closely with Euro Exim Bank as the applicant for all Bank Instruments needed in Asia.

    TOPWAY, being a distributor and a representative of several companies, accumulated a healthy market through a simple principle; BUILD, DELIVER and FOLLOW THROUGH.

  • 2016

    In 2016, TOPWAY FAR EAST INC. were duly approved by the commission and incorporated in accordance with the Corporation Code of the Philippines (Batas Pambansa Blg. 68). The company was established in the Philippines with principal address at Level 10-1 One Global Place 25th St. 5th Avenue Fort Bonifacio, Taguig City with the leadership of Mr. Angelo G. Santiago as the President, Ms. Edita E. Gutierrez as Corporate Secretary, Ms. Zeny Rose B. Malazarte as Chief Finance Officer, Ms. Angie F. Osma as Auditor and Anselmo G. Santiago Jr. as the Director of the company.

  • 2017

    In 2017, EURO EXIM CONSULTANCY LTD. was formed to facilitate import and export trading of commodities for oil and gas and other energy products, a subsidiary of TOPWAY FAREAST LTD.

  • 2020

    In the year 2020 EUROEXIM BUSINESS MANAGEMENT CONSULTANCY INC., was established in the Philippines as a branch and local company of EURO EXIM CONSULTANCY LTD. which is a subsidiary of TOPWAY FAREAST LTD., with the leadership of Atty. Pedro A. Aquino Jr. as Chairman and CEO of the company, Mr. Angelo Santiago as the President, Atty. Ricardo C. Estrabo as Chief Legal Counsel and Mr. Jesus B. Varela as the Chairman Risk & Compliance Officer.
